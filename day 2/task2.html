The Future Of BTS

BTS will continue to grow popular and continue to gain fans across the world at rapid rates. Their popularity is at all time high and they will continue to gain more fans as more people begin to accept their music. Although they have immense popularity, not all good things last forever. In Korea, there is a mandatory military service that every Korean male citizen must experience by the age of 30. There will obviously be a hiatus for the band while the members serve the mandatory military service but I think BTS has a very long way to go. It is very common for Korean male idols to return to their groups even after their two years of military service is up. They recently renewed their contract with BigHit Entertainment for another seven years but ultimately, it will be up to the group if they wish to return, work on their solo projects, or go their own ways.
